Quick Troubleshooting

  • Ads not loading? Make sure you have an active internet connection. If you opted out of tracking via App Tracking Transparency, you will still receive ads, but they may take a brief moment longer to fetch.
  • App running slowly? Try clearing your background apps or restarting your device to refresh your system's physics engine processing memory.
