Privacy Policy for Soothing Ballz

Last updated: June 12, 2026

Soothing Ballz ("we", "the app") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data is handled when you use the app.

Information We Collect

The app itself does not require an account and does not ask you for personal information. All your game settings and progress are stored locally on your device.

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to show ads. AdMob may collect and use device identifiers (such as the advertising ID), IP address, and usage data to deliver and measure ads. This data is processed by Google in accordance with its policies.

For more details, see Google's Privacy Policy and information on how Google uses data from apps that use its services.

App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

On iOS, you will be asked for permission before the app can track your activity across other companies' apps and websites. If you decline, you will still see ads, but they will be non-personalized and less relevant to you.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices & Controls

You can reset or limit ad personalization at any time in your iOS device settings by navigating to Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking or Apple Advertising.
